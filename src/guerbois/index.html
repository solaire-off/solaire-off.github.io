Guerbois is an digital art platform for meeting and contact point between artists, collectors, galleries and all the enthusiasts, to make know and promote the Art.

At the end of the 19th century the group of young artists, also known as the "Batignolles Group", that has contested the official painting of the period, was reunited every Thursday night in a café in Paris, called "Café Guerbois".

That meetings gave to birth the impressionist movement, at the origin of the contemporary art.

Fine art is that in which the hand, the head, and the heart of man go together.

John Ruskin
